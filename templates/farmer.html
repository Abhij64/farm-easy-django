Overview of Common Service Centres (CSC)

Common Service Centres (CSCs) are a strategic cornerstone of India's e-Governance initiative. These centres act as access points for various Government-to-Citizen (G2C) services, bridging the digital divide in rural and urban areas. By providing citizens with essential services like Aadhaar, banking, insurance, and digital literacy, CSCs ensure equitable access to resources and simplify service delivery.
