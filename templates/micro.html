Welcome to FarmEasy Microfinance

Microfinance is a vital financial service that provides small loans to farmers who might not have access to traditional banking. These loans empower small-scale farmers by giving them the opportunity to invest in resources that help increase their productivity, such as seeds, equipment, and fertilizers.

Eligibility Criteria

Must be a small-scale farmer with land ownership or lease agreement.

Must have a valid proof of identity and address.

Must have a good credit history (if applicable).

Must provide a viable farming plan or project proposal for loan utilization.

Age must be between 18 and 60 years.

Interest Rates and Repayment Terms

Interest Rates

The interest rate for loans is between 7% to 15%, depending on the type of loan and applicant’s credit score.

Repayment Period

Loans are to be repaid over a period of 12 to 60 months, based on the loan type and agreement.

Repayment Frequency

Repayment can be made monthly, quarterly, or annually, depending on the borrower’s preference and loan type.

Early Repayment

Early repayment is allowed without penalty, providing flexibility to the borrowers to pay off their loans faster.

Loan Application Process

Apply
